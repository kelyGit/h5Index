公司简介

        深圳市中润泰兴新型建材有限公司从事建筑声学隔声系统产品的研发、生产和工程施工的高新技术企业通过不断的努力与科技创新，研发设计出一系列隔音、构造体系与相应的绿色环保隔音胶凝砂浆、隔音涂料、轻质墙板。公司致力于为大型房地产商、大型建筑总包商、政府部门提供高品质的隔音胶凝产品和工程施工服务，公司是目前国内隔音胶凝领域技术最优、规模最大、市场占有率最高的领先企业。 公司以‘节能环保,绿色建筑’为时代责任，始终坚持以客户为根本的原则，为客户提供同行最先进、优质一体化的产品及服务，为绿色建筑节能事业再创辉煌！

 
 
  Shenzhen Zhongrun Taixing New Building Materials Co. , Ltd. is engaged in the research and development, production and construction of building acoustic insulation system products, design a series of sound insulation, structural system and the corresponding green environmental sound insulation cement mortar, sound insulation paint, light wall panel. The company is committed to providing high-quality sound insulation gel products and construction services for large real estate developers, large general contractors and government departments, at present, the company is the leading enterprise with the best technology, the largest scale and the highest market share in the field of sound insulation gelation. The company to 'energy conservation, environmental protection, green building' for the responsibility of the times, always adhere to the customer-based principle, to provide customers with the most advanced, high-quality integrated products and services, green building energy-saving business to create brilliant!
